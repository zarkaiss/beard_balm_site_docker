About us

Welcome to Beard Brothers Better Beard Balm, your number one source for all things beard related.

We're dedicated to giving you the very best beard balm and wax, with a focus on unique organic products, customer service, and dependability. Founded in 2019 by Darrell Ray, Beard Brothers has come a long way from its beginnings in Western North Carolina. When Darrell Ray first started out, his passion for beard grooming drove him to create a better beard balm and wax, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the Unitied States, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly wing of the beard industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
